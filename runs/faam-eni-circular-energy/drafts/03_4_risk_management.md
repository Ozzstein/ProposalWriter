# 3.4 Risk Management

The project has identified, assessed, and defined mitigation strategies for the critical risks that could affect achievement of financial close, entry into operation, and sustained GHG emission avoidance during the monitoring period. The risk register below covers technical, operational, supply chain, regulatory, market, and financial risk categories. Detailed risk analysis is provided in the Feasibility Study annex and the Business Plan annex.

## Overall Risk Assessment

The project's overall risk profile is **medium**, which is typical for an industrial-scale manufacturing project at the pre-FID stage. The highest individual risk (R1, calcination scale-up) is managed through the piloting-before-procurement strategy and the phased production ramp-up. The digital twin itself serves as a risk mitigation instrument: by providing real-time process visibility and predictive quality monitoring, the DT reduces the probability and impact of technical risks R1-R3 during the operational phase [CLM-007].

No identified risk is assessed as unmitigable, and no risk threatens the fundamental technical or economic viability of the project. The risk register will be maintained as a living document, reviewed quarterly by the Technical Committee, and updated at each work package milestone.

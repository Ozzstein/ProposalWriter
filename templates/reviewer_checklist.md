# Proposal Review Checklist

## Scientific Rigor
- [ ] Hypotheses are clearly stated and testable
- [ ] Methods are appropriate for the research questions
- [ ] Sample sizes / power analysis justified
- [ ] Controls and comparisons adequate
- [ ] Alternative approaches discussed
- [ ] Potential pitfalls addressed with mitigations
- [ ] Timeline is realistic

## Evidence & Claims
- [ ] All technical claims are supported by evidence
- [ ] Citations are appropriate and sufficient
- [ ] No unsupported claims (or they are marked as assumptions)
- [ ] State of the art is accurately represented
- [ ] Novelty claims are defensible

## Alignment with Call
- [ ] All required sections present
- [ ] Evaluation criteria addressed
- [ ] Scoring criteria mapped to proposal content
- [ ] Eligibility requirements met
- [ ] Budget within allowed range

## Compliance
- [ ] Page/word limits met per section
- [ ] Formatting requirements followed
- [ ] All mandatory attachments listed
- [ ] Deadline noted and achievable

## Writing Quality
- [ ] Clear and concise language
- [ ] Consistent terminology throughout
- [ ] No contradictions between sections
- [ ] Logical flow within and across sections
- [ ] Appropriate level for expert reviewers
- [ ] Active voice preferred
